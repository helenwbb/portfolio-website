My Story

Hello! My name is Helen and I am a recently certified UI / UX designer with a specialization in Front-End Programming for designers living in the Northern Virginia area. Living on a mountain, I am inspired by nature’s simplicity and hope to translate its intuitive concepts into my own designs. I have a passion to create effortless solutions within interfaces that not only make the interaction with the UI fun, but memorable for consumers, creating lasting connections between users and brands.

Before finding UI / UX Design, I worked in event planning, and before that, customer service positions while I studied for my Bachelor’s in Marketing. I always enjoyed my work, but I always felt the need for a more creative option. After stumbling upon a "Day in the Life" video about UI on YouTube, I decided to look into it more. The more I learned about UI, the more I could see myself working in the field as it combined my skills in marketing, customer service, and market research, as well as my love for design. After realizing this, I decided to take the plunge to get certified. Thanks for CareerFoundry, I am confident in my skills and feel it is only up from here! When I am not watching UI videos or designs on Behance or Dribbble, I like to write and record music, practice yoga, and grow my collection of houseplants. Nice to meet you!
