About Me

My Story

Hello! My name is Helen and I am a UI Designer currently living in the Northern Virginia area. Living on a mountain, I am inspired by nature’s simplicity and hope to translate its intuitive intrinsic concepts into my own designs. I have a passion to create effortless solutions within interfaces that not only make the interaction with the UI fun, but memorable, creating lasting connections between users and brands. I started my UI journey after the pandemic hit. Before finding UI I worked in event planning, and before that, customer service positions while I studied for my Bachelor’s in Marketing. I always enjoyed my work, but I always felt the need for a more creative option. After stumbling upon a "Day in the Life" video about UI on YouTube on day, I decided to look into it more. The more I learned about UI, the more I could see myself working in the field as it combined my skills in marketing, customer service, and market research, as well as my love for design.

After realizing this, I decided to take the plunge to get certified. Thanks for CareerFoundry, I am confident in my skills and feel it is only up from here! When I am not watching UI videos or looking up new UI art on Behance or Dribbble, I like to write and record relaxing indie music, practice yoga, and grow my collection of houseplants. Nice to meet you!
