Beautify

Beautify is a luxury cosmetics shopping app with an easy navigation that was inspired by simple customer journey maps to unite and communicate its purpose throughout all its wireframes / screens.

User flow testing and multiple iterations of the wireframes resulted in a clear, concise flow that allows Beautify to fulfill its goal of an easy navigation to help customers find exactly what they are looking for in a timely manner.

Creating Beautify

To appeal to the immediate gratification needs of the luxury consumer, Beautify was centered around four principles: the ability to have a shopping experience anywhere, swift checkout, easy searching filters, and a fast sign up. To do this, however, Beautify also had to also be elegant, simple, and clean from a UI perspective. To visualize all these theories coming together, a rough draft of user flowcharts was created: One for an easy sign up, easily accessible products, and one for checkout with many payment options.

Once the flows were down, wireframes were created for each flow.

With the wireframes finished, A/B and live testing was conducted. Participants were asked about the app's layout and checkout flow through the use of a working prototype to gain insight on any changes needed to be made.

Intitial Prototype

Testing revealed the original colorstory lacked contrast and the icons, while easy to press, were a tad too large. Thanks to testing, the icons were amended and the old colorstory reinvented. To add contrast and keep the original message of the app, which is luxury, a black, white, and gold with a royal purple accent were implemented as the new colorstory.

After these changes were made, testing was conducted again. This time, the app was easy to read and access for users. With the colors and icon size out of the way, users noted the streamlined process. After testing, the final prototype and mockups were made.
